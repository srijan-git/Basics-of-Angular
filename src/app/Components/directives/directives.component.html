Directives

Directives are classes that add additional behavior to elements in an Angular Application.

Types of Diretives

What are Structural Directives?

Structural directives changes appearance of DOM by adding or removing elements.

What are Attribute Directives?

Attribute directives changes appearance or behaviour of an element. elements.
